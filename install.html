Install SolarToolHub on your phone

Get one-tap access from your home screen — free, no app store, no download required.

📱 On Android (Chrome)

  1. Open solartoolhub in the Chrome browser
  2. Tap the three dots (⋮) in the top-right corner
  3. Tap "Install app" or "Add to Home screen" — on some devices these are the same option and both work the same way
  4. Confirm by tapping "Install" or "Add"

The SolarToolHub icon will appear on your home screen and open like a regular app, without the browser address bar.

🍎 On iPhone (Safari)

  1. Open solartoolhub in the Safari browser (must be Safari, not Chrome, for this to work on iOS)
  2. Tap the Share button (the square with an arrow pointing up, at the bottom of the screen)
  3. Scroll down and tap "Add to Home Screen"
  4. Tap "Add" in the top-right corner

The icon will appear on your home screen just like any other app.

Why install it?

  • Opens instantly, full-screen, without typing the address every time
  • Works exactly like the website — same calculators, same guides
  • Completely free, and doesn't take up much storage space
